My Work

Travis County Responsive Redesign

A lesson in information architecture.

Challenge: Can you, as a resident of Travis County, quickly navigate traviscountytx.gov and find information about your voter registration (without using Google search)?

PROJECT OVERVIEW

My Responsibilities: UX/UI Research, Analysis, and Design

Timeline: 6 weeks

Objective: Redesign the Travis County homepage and navigation to be responsive and efficient.

Design Process: Heuristic Evaluation, Proto-Persona, Geurrilla Usability Test, Redline & Annotate, Card Sort, Sitemap, UI Style Guide, Prototype, Test, Iterate

MY UX SKILLS

User Research

People have always fascinated me. I prefer to read real stories about real people. I love learning about people and understanding their motivations and challenges. I enjoy observing, listening, and asking the bigger questions. My curiosity, compassion and natural ability to empathize with others is my strength.

User Interface Design

UI design is a creative challenge that is in constant iteration according to user research and business goals. I enjoy creating user experiences that are fun and engaging, simple and elegant, and most of all easy on everybody. Creative problem solving is fun when we can explore, play and build on top of each other's ideas with no fear of making mistakes.

Web Development

I studied computer science in college and learned the theory and practice of software development and logical/analytical thinking. I found myself more interested in the look and feel of things than what's under the hood, but I have a great appreciation for developers and understand how they think. Front-end skills include HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and Angular.
