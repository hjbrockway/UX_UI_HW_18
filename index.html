DIVE INTO THE WORK

Travis County Responsive Redesign

A lesson in information architecture.

Challenge: Can you, as a resident of Travis County, quickly navigate traviscountytx.gov and find information about your voter registration (without using Google search)?

PROJECT OVERVIEW

My Responsibilities: UX/UI Research, Analysis, and Design

Timeline: 6 weeks

Objective: Redesign the Travis County homepage and navigation to be responsive and efficient.

Design Process: Heuristic Evaluation, Proto-Persona, Geurrilla Usability Test, Redline & Annotate, Card Sort, Sitemap, UI Style Guide, Prototype, Test, Iterate
